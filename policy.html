Commission Policy

Terms and conditions for project commissions

To ensure a fair and secure working relationship, please review the following terms before initiating a project:

Upfront Payment Requirement

For first-time clients, a 50% upfront payment is required prior to beginning any work. This policy is in place due to previous instances of time being wasted and work being misused.

Full Ownership Upon Completion

Upon receipt of full payment (100%), the client will receive complete ownership and rights to the commissioned work. For example, if the commission is a combat system, the client will have full control and usage rights over that system.

Minimum Project Value

All commissions must meet a minimum value of R$5,000 Robux or $10 USD. Projects below this threshold will not be accepted.

By proceeding with a commission, you acknowledge and agree to these terms. Thank you for your understanding and professionalism.
